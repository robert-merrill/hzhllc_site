About

HZH, LLC is a Utah-based consulting and software firm at the intersection of mental health strategy, talent acquisition, and proprietary technology development. We partner with enterprise organizations and mission-driven nonprofits to design smarter systems, build custom tools, and solve complex human capital challenges.
